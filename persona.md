## Digest Format Preferences

- **Summary length:** Medium — one sentence per email is enough unless it's urgent
- **Grouping:** Group by: Needs Reply → Urgent → FYI
- **Tone of the digest:** Crisp and scannable — use bullet points, not paragraphs
- **What to include in each item:**
  - Sender name
  - Subject
  - One-line gist
  - Whether I need to reply (and suggested action if obvious)
